## Post 1: The "deadly trio" for B2B SaaS (SEO + YouTube + LinkedIn)

**Date:** March 2026 (approx.)  
**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/samdunning_this-got-us-0-2m-arr-in-2-years-a-deadly-activity-7436033746618757120-jrIg  
**Reactions:** 43 | **Comments:** 13

This got us $0–$2M+ ARR in 2 years. A deadly trio for B2B and SaaS:

1. **SEO/AEO** — Show up at the top of Google organic and AI search when dream clients need your solution now, are comparing options, are annoyed with their current vendor, or have an expensive problem you solve. Capture active demand from the ~5% of your ICP in-market today.

2. **YouTube** — Not throwing old webinars and hoping for results. Craft searchable videos sharing every aspect of your playbook, approach, strategies, product, and problems you solve. YouTube-first approach with solid packaging, hook, story, CTAs, and pacing. Focused on dream clients with intent — not traffic at all costs.

3. **LinkedIn (founder brand & paid)** — Daily founder posting to become a trusted voice. Edutainment with memes, videos, and breakdown posts. Thought leader ads retargeting site traffic from all above channels plus cold ICP lists.

These 3 channels working together are cooking demos at Breaking B2B and for many SaaS clients.
