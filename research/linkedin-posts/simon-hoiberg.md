## Post 1: How I create highly clickable YouTube thumbnails (AI workflow)

**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/simonhoiberg_youtube-flux-lora-n8n-how-i-create-activity-7297600705027035138-H5ct  
**Reactions:** 64 comments

YouTube + Flux LoRA + n8n — How I create highly clickable YouTube thumbnails.

**Old way:** Create thumbnail first. Let packaging lead the idea, script, and final edit.

**New way:** Create the video first. Then use this n8n workflow:
1. Get the video + transcript
2. Use ChatGPT to generate thumbnail concept ideas from the transcript
3. Use custom Flux LoRA model to generate thumbnails
4. (Optional) Add text on top, final touches in Photoshop

Used for last 5–10 videos with amazing results.

**Philosophy shift:** YouTube has evolved. Create videos you WANT to make, that CONVERT better, that are most VALUABLE — then let AI help you package it. Don't constrain yourself to the most "clickable" packaging first.

## Post 2: Solopreneur stack for $10K MRR Micro SaaS

**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/simonhoiberg_most-successful-founders-keep-it-dead-simple-activity-7391820487343091712-jecR  
**Reactions:** 31 comments

Most successful founders keep it dead simple. Building a $10K MRR Micro SaaS solo is possible — keep it simple:

1. **Founder Layer** — Dedicated time slot every week; consistency is key
2. **Content Layer** — Most effective low-cost marketing. Tools: ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, FeedHive. Two channels: **LinkedIn (text/image) + YouTube (video)**
3. **Product Layer** — Build with Cline/Lovable/Cursor; serve with Featurebase/Aidbase
4. **Operational Layer** — Automate with Lindy AI, n8n, Notion; delegate via Fiverr, YT Jobs

**Key insight:** LinkedIn + YouTube is all you need for founder-led content marketing at early stage.

## Post 4: Should your SaaS have an AAI (Application Agent Interface)?
