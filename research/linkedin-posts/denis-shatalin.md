## Post 1: Shiny object syndrome — Depth beats breadth

**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/denis-shatalin_shiny-object-syndrome-has-cost-me-more-than-activity-7456497287574302720-MZ3b

Shiny object syndrome has cost me more than any bad business decision I've ever made.

The same pattern shows up in B2B SaaS: a founder sees someone crushing it with a funnel, switches. Sees someone else on a different channel, switches again. Six months later — surface-level understanding of five approaches, deep expertise in none.

Over the years tested 30+ funnels: webinars, SEO, cold email, LinkedIn outbound, Google Ads, YouTube, events. Every channel works in some situations and fails in others.

**Meta ads are underrated for B2B SaaS** — but require real budget upfront, tolerance for uncertainty, and patience to let the algorithm learn.

The founders who scale fastest pick a channel, commit long enough to develop expertise, and stop looking for something easier when things get hard.

**Depth beats breadth almost every time.**
