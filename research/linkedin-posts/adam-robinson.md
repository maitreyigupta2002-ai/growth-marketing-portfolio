## Post 1: New YouTube workflow with Samu Kovacs (1 hour/week)

**Date:** ~October 2025  
**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/retentionadam_in-the-last-30-days-ive-made-4-youtube-activity-7389372943841144832-WA5o  
**Reactions:** 146 | **Comments:** 60

In the last 30 days, I've made 4 YouTube videos with Samu Kovacs. Each has 2x the views of the last and 10x my other videos. My last one drove 10 RB2B signups.

Here's my new YouTube workflow (only 1 hour/week):

1. **Running "ideas" doc** — Constantly adding when an old post should be a YT video or when there's a gap in RB2B educational content
2. **Samu picks an idea** — Asks if Adam has more content (posts, voice notes, presentations, courses)
3. **Samu writes intro, outro, and body bullets** — Sends by Friday evening. Breakthrough: Adam has so much content that Samu can nail his voice
4. **Adam spends 30 min in PowerPoint** — For teleprompter, light edits, get familiar
5. **Records in Riverside** — Sends link to Samu
6. **Samu edits**
7. **Drop video every Friday** — Traffic from newsletter + LinkedIn post

**Results:** 1,500 views per video isn't much vs. LinkedIn presence — but trend is moving right. Goal was Instantly's channel (~3,000 avg views). Was getting 100–200 views; 4 videos in, halfway there.

**Why YouTube:** Video creates deeper connection than text; repurposes content across channels; LinkedIn talking heads may hurt post performance.
