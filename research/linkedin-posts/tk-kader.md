## Post 1: Scaling GTM — 6-Step Framework for Founders in 2026

**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/tkkader_i-think-something-weird-has-happened-in-2026-activity-7441244184969342976-pUS8

I think something weird has happened in 2026 for Founder-led GTM. Founders forgot that Sales + Marketing builds a scalable Go-To-Market machine.

- **Sales is 1-on-1** — Outbound cold emails are 1-on-1 (even spraying 15,000 prospects)
- **Marketing is 1-to-many** — A single LinkedIn post can reach thousands of ideal customers

**Why you need both:**
- Outbound email: ~1.2% response rate — gets a meeting this week but won't scale
- Marketing (done right): ~40% opt-in to lead magnet, ~20% activation to book meetings, scalable ad spend
- Outbound win rate: ~10% | Inbound booked meetings: 50%+ win rate

By the time someone shows up to an inbound meeting, they already know you, trust you, and understand why you're 10X better than competition.

## Post 2: 5 GTM mistakes holding AI SaaS founders below $3M ARR

**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/tkkader_5-point-aisaas-growth-strategy-guide-exclusively-activity-7454875361089167360-zgOY

AI and SaaS founders stall not because product is weak — but because early traction hides a broken GTM:

1. No scalable GTM machine — surviving on heroic effort
2. Founder still carrying entire commercial motion — growth bottlenecked around one exhausted human
3. New customers keep asking for different things — volatility, not a system
4. Product-market fit was permission to build GTM, not the finish line

**Framework:** ICP → Manifesto → Broadway Show (GTM motions)
