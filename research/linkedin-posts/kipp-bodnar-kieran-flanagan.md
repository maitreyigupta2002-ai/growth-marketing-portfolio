## Post 2: Why I refuse to hack the algorithm (YouTube clip)

**Source:** Marketing Against the Grain YouTube  
**URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0lzFvXmGd3g

Kieran Flanagan on the tension between what the algorithm wants and what you want to create:

When plugging top-performing video titles into AI, titles started going more clickbait and ragebait. They could probably get more views with controversial packaging — but that creates brand risk.

The moment you optimize purely for algorithmic performance, you lose control of the moral values and brand you want to convey through your content.

**Insight for B2B YouTube strategy:** Distribution and packaging matter, but not at the cost of brand trust with your ICP.
