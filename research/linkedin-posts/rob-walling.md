## Post 2: 5 key takeaways from TinyFest

**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/robwalling_i-spent-25-days-in-a-room-with-50-saas-founders-activity-7292215389323673603-QZo6

Spent 2.5 days with 50 SaaS founders at TinySeed's annual gathering. Key takeaways:

1. **In-person connections are irreplaceable** — Momentum no Zoom call can match
2. **Pricing remains the ultimate lever** — Get it right, 4x revenue; get it wrong, perfect execution won't save you
3. **The SaaS playbook is evolving** — Founders sharing what's working right now
4. **Inspiration drives execution** — Perfect tactics without motivation to ship = nothing happens
5. **Real relationships unlock everything** — Slack hits different after sharing a conversation in person
