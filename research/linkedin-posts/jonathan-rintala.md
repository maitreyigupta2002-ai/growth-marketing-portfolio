## Post 2: Running a startup is hard — 7 ways to wind down

**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/jonathan-rintala_running-a-startup-is-hard-here-is-why-and-activity-7241404852382187521-DxpB  
**Posted:** ~8 months ago

Running a startup is hard. Your TODO list never stops growing — you will NEVER get it down to zero.

7 ways to deal with the pressure:
1. Always get 8 hours of sleep (Garmin tracking, >80 sleep score)
2. Don't feel guilty when you don't work
3. Activities that detach completely — triathlon, reading
4. Work out 3x/week, aim for VO2Max > 55
5. Build value outside of work — competitions, hobbies
6. Regular sauna + daily cold plunge
7. Spend time outside — hike, water, run to work

**Founder content angle:** Authentic build-in-public content about the human side of scaling a B2B SaaS with content marketing.
